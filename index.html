About Sharon Oud:

Sharon Oud is a wearer of many hats who loves to solve problems and empower people to be their best. Experienced supervisor, team leader, trainer, and escalations analyst in a global Fortune company.

She enjoys challenges and always learning. Currently working on expanding her technical expertise from troubleshooting business hardware to full-stack coding.

In her spare time she enjoys being with friends and family, reading, and gardening. She has an interest in "climate zone pushing" tropical fruiting plants with a focus on mangos.
